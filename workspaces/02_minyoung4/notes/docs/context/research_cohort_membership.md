## Split Implication

- Use `leakage_group_id = dataset::subject_id` as the grouping key for any future split.
- MU, UCSD, and UPENN include subjects with multiple NIfTI units, so unit-level random splits would leak subject information.
- Future splits should additionally report dataset, scanner vendor, field strength, age bin, sex, and target balance.

## Machine-Readable Outputs

- `research_cohort_membership.csv`: one row per subject with eligibility flags.
- `research_cohort_summary.csv`: cohort-by-dataset eligible counts, positives/negatives where applicable, and pre-model gates.
- `research_leakage_group_audit.csv`: subject grouping and multi-unit leakage summary.

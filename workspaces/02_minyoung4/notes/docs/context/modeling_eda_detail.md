## Key Takeaways

1. The strongest all-dataset imaging baseline remains core structural MRI.
2. IDH is the strongest cross-dataset supervised molecular label, but dataset/scanner confounding is large.
3. MGMT is feasible as a second-stage task with lower coverage and more missing/conflicting labels.
4. Survival and progression outcomes are not pool-ready without explicit time-origin and censor harmonization.
5. Diffusion/perfusion should be treated as UCSD+UPENN focused, not all-consortium common input.
6. UPENN DICOM and histopath are overlapping/subset representations and need separate leakage controls.

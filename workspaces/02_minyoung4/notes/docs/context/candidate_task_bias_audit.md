## Interpretation

- IDH is still the best first supervised candidate by coverage, but its positive rate varies strongly by dataset and age/scanner strata.
- MGMT has lower coverage and multiple small-cell strata, so it should follow after a first IDH protocol.
- Any future split must be subject-grouped and must report dataset, scanner vendor, field strength, age bin, sex, and target balance.
- Leave-one-consortium-out reporting is important because pooled random performance may reflect site/label shortcuts.

## Notes

- `canonical_manifest.csv` intentionally includes both UPENN NIfTI scan rows and UPENN DICOM series rows; they overlap and must not be split independently.
- MU molecular labels were decoded from the local data dictionary: 0/1 are generally negative/positive, higher codes are unknown/indeterminate/other according to field.
- UPENN diagnosis is marked `gbm_collection`, not an observed per-row diagnosis variable.
- Histopath rows use `radiology_mapping.csv`; several slides have missing `RadiologyID`, so they cannot all be safely linked to imaging without additional curation.

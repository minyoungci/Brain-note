## Conservative Takeaways

1. The safest cross-dataset image task is structural MRI representation/pretraining or downstream modeling with age/sex covariates.
2. Structural+segmentation is nearly complete, but one UCSD BraTS test segmentation is zero-byte and must be excluded or restored.
3. IDH is the strongest molecular label candidate; subject-level conflict rows should be excluded or resolved before split creation.
4. MGMT is feasible but lower coverage and more missing/indeterminate labels make it a second-stage task.
5. Survival modeling should not be pooled until time origin and censor semantics are normalized.
6. Advanced imaging tasks are mainly UCSD+UPENN, not all-consortium baselines.
